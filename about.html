Ceteris Paribus

About

Ceteris Paribus is an entry in the second Practice Club Jam, where the theme is “Structures in Choice-based Games”. The inspiration for Ceteris Paribus came from 100,000 Years, Portal, and The Linear RPG.

A number of people have influenced or contributed to Ceteris Paribus. Thanks to:

  • I. D. Millington for Undum,
  • Alberto Pino for the cover photo,
  • Milos Mirkovic for the icons,
  • Momez (Mouloud) for the background texture, and
  • Andrew Plotkin for organizing the jam.
